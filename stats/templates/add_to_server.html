Adding MuffinBot

Step 1: Add the bot to your server

Click this link!
Verify that you approve of the permissions, and add it to your server.


Step 2: Perform basic setup

Visit the dashboard, select your server, and select which channels you want the bot to be able to read. Anyone with "Manage Server" permissions may perform this setup.


Step 3: Start the bot

Use the /start command in any channel that MuffinBot can read, and it will begin reading your messages.
To confirm that the command has worked, select your server from the dashboard to see its statistics. Once the bot has started, anyone who is in your server may view the server's statistics by logging into this website and selecting it from the dashboard.


Step 4 (Optional): Collect statistics on the server's history

If you'd like, you may use the history command, which collects statistics on all of your server's past messages. It may take a while for the bot to complete this process.
